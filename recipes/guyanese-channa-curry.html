Guyanese Channa Curry

Chick pea or channa curry is a very popular in Guyana and Trinidad where the cuisine has a heavy Indian influence. This comes as no surprise as itâ€™s often served with some of the most populist dishes like doubles and roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15oz) chick peas
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon green seasoning
  • 2 tablespoon water
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. In small bowl mix curry powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin, green seasoning and 2 tablespoon water to form a paste
  2. In a medium size pot, heat oil over medium fire. When oil is hot add curry paste and onion and cook for 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. If the paste becomes dry, about a tablespoon of water to prevent it from burning. Next add chick pea, salt and remaining 4 cups of water. Cook until the chick peas are tender. Add water as needed.
  4. Enjoy! Serve with rice/roti
